# Lumi AI Daily Cover Style

Use this reference when preparing the daily cover. The default cover path is now the image generation skill, with deterministic Swift assembly kept only as a fallback or layout draft.

## Objective

The cover is the day's traffic source. It must work as a thumbnail before it works as a poster.

The final hierarchy should be:

- one dominant real-news visual or generated editorial scene grounded in real source assets
- one huge main hook
- one short supporting subhead
- one optional evidence card or product badge
- Lumi as a small recurring host/brand mark

## Lumi Character Requirement

- Every cover must include Lumi.
- Lumi reference image: `/Users/dystopia/.openclaw/workspace/assets/lumi-cover-ref.png`
- Required traits: a close 藤原千花 / Chika Fujiwara style restoration: pale pink hair, large black bow accessory, warm playful expression, cheerful student-council-secretary energy, white school-uniform blouse, black shoulder straps/ribbon details, and a small readable `Lumi` chest badge.
- The image prompt may explicitly name 藤原千花 / Chika Fujiwara; the production target is a Lumi host that closely restores that character look while adding the `Lumi` badge.
- Avoid hoodie, cyberpunk outfit, VTuber streamer styling, mature influencer styling, or generic mascot look.
- Lumi should occupy no more than 15 percent of the frame.
- Place Lumi in a lower corner as a brand/host signal.
- Keep the pose and lighting adapted to the news cover, but keep the character likeness much closer than a generic pink-haired anime host.

## Text Rules

- Main hook: 6-18 Chinese visual characters, target 4-12 when possible.
- Subhead: 6-18 Chinese visual characters.
- The main hook must be readable at mobile thumbnail size.
- The cover hook and article title share the same traffic thesis but should not be the same sentence.
- Use strong concrete words: `回归`, `开战`, `接管`, `翻车`, `真能用?`, `新入口`, `成本线`, `审计来了`.
- Avoid weak labels: `今日盘点`, `AI日报`, `六条新闻`, `一文看懂`.

## Imagegen Prompt Shape

Generate a 16:9 AI news thumbnail cover.

Required:

- preserve the named daily entity or product
- preserve the main hook text exactly
- preserve the subhead text exactly
- include Lumi in a lower corner
- use one dominant source visual
- make the main text massive, high contrast, and separated from the background

Recommended visual formulas:

1. `A vs B`: two product cards or screens facing each other, central tension.
2. `Workflow changed`: real UI/workspace background with a before/after or upgraded path.
3. `Breakthrough shock`: one object or paper result made visually impossible.
4. `Risk arrived`: dark product surface with one warning/evidence card.
5. `Official drop`: product announcement visual plus host reaction.

## Visual Finish

- High contrast.
- Fewer than three text zones.
- Large type with thick outline or strong shadow.
- Background darkened or simplified.
- One clear focal path from entity -> hook -> Lumi.

## Asset Selection

Prefer:

- official hero or product images
- product screenshots with clear recognizable UI
- paper figures only if visually simple
- source visuals that explain the story in one glance

Reject as dominant background:

- tiny logos
- raw browser pages
- QR/login/search pages
- dense benchmark tables
- unreadable chart walls
- generic AI imagery unrelated to the story

## Negative Cues

- no fake platform numbers or timestamps
- no made-up awards or scores
- no fake crisis claims
- no unrelated robot brain/circuit filler
- no logo wall
- no unreadable small UI text as the main image
- no more than two large text zones
